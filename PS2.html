How to emulate PS2 games

Step 1

To get started you first need to get a physical disc drive that can connect to your PC, a USB, and a disc.

Then you need to go to https://github.com/F0bes/biosdrain and download biosdrain and follow the steps on that page. once youve done that go to https://pcsx2.net and follow the instructions on how to dump BIOS files onto your pc.

Step 2

After following the steps on github you need to go to https://pcsx2.net and download pcsx2. This is the emulator that will allow you to play PS2 games on your PC.

Step 3

Finnally you have to dump the game itself. to do this go to https://pcsx2.net and follow the instructions on how to dump your PS2 game onto your PC.

From there you can open PCSX2 and modify your settings to your liking and then load a game you dumped.
