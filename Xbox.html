Step 1

First you need to get the BIOS and have the xbox game disk that you want to emulate. to get the bios go to https://xemu.app/ and press "documentation" then go to "required files" and download files.

Step 2

Next you need to download Xemu from the site above. install it then go to system settings in the app and open all the files you downloaded and put them into Xemu.

Step 3

Finnaly you haver to get your games. to do this you can just search something similar to "original xbox game roms" and download those and make sure the files are .iso or .xiso so they can work. then open Xemu and press "machines" then press "load disc" and then find you game file and open it and you should be able to play.
